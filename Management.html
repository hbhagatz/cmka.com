Mr. Asad Rizwi, Chief Executive Officer

Asad Rizwi established Currency Market Associates (Pvt.) Ltd. “CMKA” in 1994 and since then holds the position of Chief Executive Officer. In 1983, he began his career with National Bank of Oman, Muscat and moved to Pakistan in 1987 to join Chase Manhattan Bank, Pakistan as Vice President and Country Treasurer. He worked for over 7 years with Chase before its closure of Pakistan operation. Asad has been associated with the financial services industry for over 30 year and has diverse range of experience as an accomplished financial markets professional in foreign exchange and interbank money market brokerage business. Asad developed the business of CMKA and regularly contributes his views to a large audience of professionals on global and domestic economy, Forex and Gold. His various articles and research material on currency outlook, charting and overall views on financial markets are published in Pakistan’s leading financial newspaper “Business Recorder” and are also referred to business schools’ students in Pakistan. In 2009, Asad assisted SECP, as a consultant in highlighting regulatory gaps and possible conflicts under a World Bank’s Credit Rating Agencies project. He also anchors TV business talk-shows and is considered as the leading analyst on AAJ TV for Pakistan’s Economy and Financial Markets.

Mr. Zohair Ali, Joint-Chief Executive Officer

Zohair Ali has been working with CMKA for last 8 years. In 1976, he began his career with Pakistan’s largest bank - Habib Bank Ltd. where he worked for 38 years until his retirement in 2014. After completion of his initial banking training, he worked as Head of Export Refinance cell and in 1983, moved to Money Market trading desk. In 1995, HBL had established formal treasury set-up where Zohair worked as Head of Forex. In 1997, he became the Chief Dealer. In 2010, he held the position of General Manager (EVP) of HBL Treasury and was acting Treasurer of bank in 2011. Zohair had an accomplished professional career and is considered as one of the most experienced specialists of Forex and Money Market businesses in Pakistan. He enjoys excellent market standing on his knowledge and skills of over three decades witnessing the transformation of treasury business in Pakistan.

Mr. Habib Tejani – Director and Chief Financial Officer ‘CFO’

Mr. Tejani holding ACMA qualification is a founder member of CMKA, working for over 28 years as Chief Financial Officer ‘CFO’. Prior to joining CMKA in 1994, he worked with a large foreign owned shipping agent, M/S Cooper and Company (Pvt) Ltd., as Finance Manager and was a key player in the tremendous growth and success of the company in Pakistan for over 10 years. At CMKA, Mr. Tejani oversees administrative, financial, audits, reporting and compliance improvements. He also collaborates with business heads to ensure overall success of CMKA and have developed and utilized financial control systems in accordance with sound management procedures and financial practices.

Mr. Khalid Jamil – General Manager

Khalid Jamil joined CMKA in 2016. Khalid has mostly been associated with the financial industry with extensive commercial banks’ Treasury and investment banking experience. During his 30 years´ experience in Kuwait, Pakistan and Turkey, he has worked in a diverse range of senior position roles. He began his career in Kuwait with an investment bank and moved to Pakistan in 1992. He was Country Treasurer of Credit Agricole (The Global French Bank) Pakistan for ten years. He was also the founder for setting-up interbank Money Market brokerage business of a leading brokerage house in Karachi. He holds a professional diploma of AIBD Zurich/ICMA London, specializing in international bonds, currencies and capital market businesses.

Mr. Fareeduddin Asif - Head of Foreign Exchange

Asif joined CMKA in 2018 and has 21 years of interbank foreign exchange brokerage business experience as Head of dealing desk. He has also worked for over 15 years with a leading bank in Foreign Exchange contract department. He has rich experience in handling structured interbank Forex brokerage business and he brings with him good market knowledge. Mr. Mohammad Ismail - Head of Money Market

He has rich experience of 20 years in interbank Money Market brokerage business and brings with him good market professional knowledge and reputation. Mr. Adnan Latif Khan, Senior Dealer - Money Market Brokerage Desk
